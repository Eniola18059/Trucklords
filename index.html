About Trucklord

Located in Oshodi, Lagos, Trucklord sources proven Howo & Sino heavy trucks and performs mechanical, chassis, electrical and cosmetic refurbishment so each vehicle leaves our yard roadworthy.

Our skilled technicians conduct full engine overhauls, suspension renewal, electrical inspections, and structural reinforcement where necessary. We keep a strong focus on safety and reliability — every truck receives a road-test and final quality check before it is cleared for sale.
We offer inspection options, live video walk-throughs for remote buyers, and nationwide delivery with insured logistics. For fleet buyers we provide tailored maintenance and parts-sourcing support to keep your vehicles operational for longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the condition of the trucks?
+
All trucks are fully inspected and refurbished to high standards. We replace worn parts, recondition engines where necessary, and run road tests before delivery.
Do you deliver nationwide?
+
Yes — we deliver across Nigeria. We offer insured logistics and can arrange transport from Oshodi to your state.
Can I inspect before purchase?
+
Yes. Inspections can be arranged at our yard in Oshodi. We can also do live video walk-throughs and provide inspection reports for remote buyers.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept bank transfers and verified escrow services. For local purchases, we can discuss convenient payment plans — contact us on WhatsApp to discuss.
Do you offer after-sales support?
+
Yes — we provide post-purchase support for maintenance, spare-parts sourcing, and advice on upkeep.
